Bunzana

2020

This is a team project at school, we have to create, for one
semester. A fake ad with two randomly picked subjects (shampoo
and banana).
I was in charge of modeling and texturing the coconut and aloes
and modeling the shampoo bottle.The texture of
the shampoo bottle was made by Lianne Chagnon
and the water simulation made by Jasmine Matos.

Software used:

Houdini/Redshift, Maya, Substance Painter, Photoshop.
